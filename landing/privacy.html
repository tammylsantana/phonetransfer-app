Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2025

Overview

PhoneTransfer ("we", "our", or "the app") is a phone transfer guide that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. PhoneTransfer is an on-device utility that:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, messages, or any phone data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertising
  • Does not share data with third parties

Local Storage

PhoneTransfer stores your checklist progress and preferences (such as dark mode setting) locally on your device using browser/app local storage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by resetting the app.

In-App Purchases

PhoneTransfer uses Apple's App Store for payment processing. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data. Their privacy policy is available at: revenuecat.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

PhoneTransfer does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@phonetransfer.app
